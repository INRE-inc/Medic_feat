# Medical Form 
For interational clients or thoes that simply do not have the time for a scheduled visit, this form helps give the clinical team vital information for a first impression. Although this cannot replace an actual consultaion, this form allows for quick consultation sessions. 

## Important Information 
A few key information is requested on this form, such as basic Bio-information and some contact information. Apart from this, there are a couple of lifestyle question that can be key to the treatment plan proposed, this information can also be used during research studies to help improve results.

## Tech stack 
Html, CSS, Javasript, PHP 

## Price Etimator
This feature enable potential clients to be aware of the possible cost implications of thier treatments and procedures, although prices may change this rough estimate allows for clinets to plan ahead.
